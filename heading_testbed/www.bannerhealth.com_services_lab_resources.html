Lab Resources

Back To Lab

Banner Health lab services help support your health and well-being with accurate, timely results for you and your loved ones. We know that getting a lab test can bring up questions, and we’re here to guide you through every step.

Whether you need routine testing or preoperative lab work, our experts provide the information and care you need.

Frequently asked questions about lab

From preparing for your test to understanding your results, we’re here to answer your questions about the lab testing process.

Learn more by visiting our lab FAQ or speaking with your health care provider.

Lab tests for surgery

Routine preoperative lab tests help ensure you are ready for surgery and can reduce the risk of complications. Your health care provider may order tests such as:

  • Blood work
  • Urinalysis
  • Electrocardiogram (EKG)

Learn more about the lab tests your provider may request before surgery and how to prepare for them.

Need more information?

If you have questions about your testing, preparation instructions or lab results, contact your health care provider.

Schedule your lab test today at one of our convenient Banner Health locations and get results you can trust.
