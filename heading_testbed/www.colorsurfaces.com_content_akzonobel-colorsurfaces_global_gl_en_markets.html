Our Markets

AUTOMOTIVE

As a design team, we collaborate with Automotive OEMs in a range of ways, from sharing color & material trend information as a starting point for new color development to digital tools to help designers create the perfect look for their project.
VISIT AUTOMOTIVE WEBSITE

CONSUMER ELECTRONICS

For Consumer Electronics, we work directly with the Color Material Finishes (CMF) teams within various markets like wireless, IT and home appliances. We offer them accurate trend information and digital tools to support the initial stage of new color development.
VISIT CONSUMER ELECTRONICS WEBSITE

AEROSPACE

Our color and material research is also relevant for design teams within the aerospace business. For the cabin interiors market we translate our ColorSurfaces trend analysis into an inspiring range within the INTURA collection.
