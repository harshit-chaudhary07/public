PAYMENT INFO
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Can I pay using multiple payment methods?
No, we currently only accept one payment method per order.

However, if you need to use more than one payment method, please contact our customer assistance line to assist you with creating this order with multiple payment methods.
How will the payment appear on my bank statement?
Your payment will appear on your statement as a charge from “Neuropasil LLC” or “Neuropasil Products.”
Do you charge sales tax?
Sales tax is applied according to state and local regulations. You’ll see the applicable tax displayed at checkout.
ORDER & RETURN INFO
Can I track my order after it’s placed?
Yes, once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. Use this number to track your package on the carrier’s website.
What is your return policy?
We offer a lifetime return policy. If you are not satisfied with your Neuropasil product, you can return it at any time for a full refund or replacement.
How do I return a product?
To return a product, contact our customer service team with your order details. They will provide you with instructions on how to process your return.
What should I do if my product arrives damaged?
If your product arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
