FAQs


Q1: When should I start shopping for back-to-school supplies?


A: Its best to start shopping for school supplies and uniforms 46 weeks before the school year begins. This gives you time to find everything you need without the last-minute rush or risk of items being out of stock.


Q2: What should be included on a back-to-school supply list?


A: Typical back-to-school supply lists include notebooks, folders, pencils, pens, crayons or markers, erasers, glue, scissors, and a backpack. Some schools also require headphones, calculators, or specific art suppliesso be sure to check your schools list.


Q3: Are school uniforms mandatory at all schools?


A: No, not all schools require uniforms. However, many public and private schools do. Its important to check your schools dress code policy before purchasing clothing for the new school year.
