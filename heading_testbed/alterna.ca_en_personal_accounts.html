Banking on your terms.

Whether you have a little money or a lot, we can always help you find a smarter way to make your money work for you.

From convenient everyday chequing accounts to high-interest savings accounts, we make it easy to manage your money and save for the future. Open an Alterna personal bank account and start benefiting today.

Chequing Accounts

Simply Chequing

 

Choose a convenient banking package that suits your needs. If you have more than one chequing account, you can choose a different package for each account.



  • Easy access with online, mobile app and telephone banking
  • Deposit a cheque on the go using our mobile banking app on your smartphone or tablet
  • Debit card to pay for purchases or withdraw cash
  • Surcharge-free access to over 43,000 ATMs in North America with THE EXCHANGE® Network in Canada and Allpoint Network in the US
  • Safety and security—eligible deposits are insured up to the maximum amount covered by the Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario (FSRA)

US Dollar Chequing*

 

Enjoy the convenience of banking in US dollars and control when to convert your funds.

Overdraft Protection Plans

Add overdraft protection to your Alterna chequing account and enjoy the peace of mind of knowing you’ll have access to extra funds when you need them. No longer worry about your account becoming overdrawn if you accidentally don’t have enough money in your account to clear outstanding cheques, pre-authorized debits and debit card transactions. Your overdraft protection will automatically come into effect to cover any shortfall up to your approved overdraft limit. Choose from our Monthly or Pay-Per-Use plans.
