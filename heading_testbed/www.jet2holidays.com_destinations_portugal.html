About Portugal
Text
Holidays to Portugal have got it all – charm, cuisine, culture! The Algarve's just one great example, with excellent golf, Blue Flag shores and tonnes of family-friendly attractions too. And with coastal promenades and gorgeously green golf courses, it’s no wonder it’s so popular with active types. Stunning weather and stellar views mean it’s the perfect place to practise your swing all year round. Want to experience Portugal’s swanky side? Make your way to Vilamoura for dining in yacht-filled marinas, sipping drinks in cool cocktail bars and kicking back on golden beaches. Why not wine and dine in Alvor? This resort takes things at a more relaxed place, so if it’s a fly-and-flop holiday you’re after, you won’t be disappointed. For Portugal with an island twist, look to Madeira. Its lush botanical gardens and epic hiking trails make it a magnet for explorers. There’s also a great calendar of events on offer, from firework shows to flower festivals. Whichever patch of the country you plump for, all parts take pride in their love for good food, good wine and good times. Cinnamon-sprinkled custard tarts, grilled sardines, crisp wine and velvety port are all national treasures, so sampling them is a rite of passage for any holiday to Portugal.

All about the beaches

If you’re looking for huge, golden-sand beaches and first-class sporting facilities, head to the Algarve. For a quieter pace of life, Madeira is the place to be.

Great for golfers

Portugal is one of Europe’s most popular golfing destinations, with Algarve golf holidays drawing crowds year round. It's home to more than 40 greens, with a handful ranked among the best in the world. And thanks to the stunning weather and stellar views, you can enjoy a game any time of the year.

Sights, sights, sights

Want something more than fly and flop? Away from the fairways and oceanfronts, Portugal holidays offer plenty to do. The Algarve’s waterparks, pretty old towns and surfing spots are perfect for families. Sleepy Madeira, meanwhile, is all about the great outdoors.
