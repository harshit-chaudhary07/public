Screen repair  

Is a cracked or damaged display keeping you from seeing clearly? Visit an Asurion or uBreakiFix store near you, and let our Google Pixel 3a screen replacement experts help you repair or replace your screen.  

Battery replacement  

Battery replacement is quick and easy. First we start with a full diagnostic to make sure you don’t have another underlying problem. Over time, batteries naturally lose their ability to charge. The older your model, the more likely the battery is to die. Don’t worry, though—we have the highest-quality batteries waiting for you at your local Asurion or uBreakiFix store.  

Water damage checkup 

Your Google Pixel 3a is a supersmart device, but one thing it can’t do is swim. So if your phone has come in contact with water, you need to take it to an expert as soon as possible. If you don’t, corrosion can set in and short-circuit your device. We’ll run a complete water-damage diagnostic to see how extensive the damage is and whether we can revive your Google Pixel 3a. Forget the bag of rice—stop by the nearest Asurion or uBreakiFix store for help.  

Charging port repair   

If the charging port on your Google Pixel 3a gets clogged with dirt, sand, or dust, you may not be able to charge it. Try gently cleaning it with a cotton swab or toothpick. If that doesn’t work, visit your nearest Asurion or uBreakiFix store for a free checkup, and we’ll get your phone back up and running quickly.
