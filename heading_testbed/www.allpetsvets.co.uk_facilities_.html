Comprehensive veterinary facilities in North Wales

To give you and your pets the best experience possible, we continue to invest in our team, and the equipment and facilities at our Rhyl and Prestatyn clinics.

This includes making sure our vets have access to a wide range of up-to-date diagnostic aids such as ultrasound and digital x-ray machines.

We also have a cardiology department at our Prestatyn practice to treat heart conditions and circulatory system disease.

Veterinary facilities for you & your pets

We understand that the thought of putting your beloved pet through surgery, blood tests, or hospitalisation can be worrying. At AllPets Vets, our friendly and caring team will guide you through all of the options available to you in a calm environment.

We’ve made sure our customer facilities have been designed to keep you comfortable whenever you visit. You will find our specially selected range of healthcare products on display and plenty of pet-related reading materials in our waiting areas. Just ask our experienced team for advice and they’ll be happy to help.

We also have spacious consulting rooms for a relaxed appointment with our veterinary team.

For the comfort of all our patients, we have dedicated pet care wards to keep cats and dogs separate – this also helps to minimise any unnecessary stress.

We even have a separate cat lounge waiting area at Prestatyn!
